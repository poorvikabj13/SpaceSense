## Design Tips for Your PPT:

1. **Color Scheme:**
   - Primary: Dark blue (#020b1c)
   - Accent: Teal/Cyan (#4ecdc4)
   - Alert: Red (#ff6b6b)

2. **Fonts:**
   - Headings: Montserrat Bold
   - Body: Open Sans

3. **Visuals:**
   - Use screenshots from your working app
   - Add icons for features
   - Include the demo video

4. **Keep It Simple:**
   - Max 5 bullet points per slide
   - Large, readable text
   - High contrast

5. **Tell a Story:**
   - Problem → Solution → Impact
   - Show, don't just tell
